Hair Care

Together we can tackle all your hair problems and create a better tomorrow!

Advanced aesthetics is a one stop solution to all your hair related problems. Here, at advance aesthetics we believe in delivering the best results according to the patients wishes and satisfaction.

Advanced trichosure treatment -

Trichosure is a word derived from trichoscopy, which means evaluation of hair and scalp and diagnosing diseases related to them. During this process the hair and scalp structures are scrutinized carefully and visualized at many-fold magnification ranging from 10-fold to 70-fold.

No needle mesotherapy treatment -

As the name suggests no needle mesotherapy is a technique used to deliver the cosmetic products with bioactive ingredients purported to have medical benefits to the "meso" (middle) layer of your skin without using any injection making it a feasible option for needle-phobic people.

Using ultrasound technology scalp nourishing additives such as medicines, vitamin B, Co-enzymes and copper peptide are imparted which simulate hair growth and imporves hair fibres giving excellent visible results.

Hair transplant treatment -

Hair transplant is a process of moving hair from one part of the body to the bald area of the head.

Hair from side or back of the head are generally transplanted to the front or top of the head. In this procedure, grafts containing hair follicles that are genetically resistant to balding are transplanted to the bald scalp, eyelashes, eyebrows, beard hair, chest hair or pubic hair.
